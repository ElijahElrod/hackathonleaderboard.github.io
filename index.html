About


This website was made to showcase the greatest hackathoners of our time. It contains a ranking system for hackathon winners based on the number of awards won at hackathons. This was made by a team of fellow hackathoners: Elijah Elrod, Jim Mirzakhalov, and Willie McClinton. Feel free to contact us with any recommendations for future updates. Let the hacking begin.
