About


This website was made to showcase the greatest collegiate hackathoners of our time. It contains a ranking system for hackathon winners based on the number of awards won in hackathons. We calculated the amount of awards won by crawling Devpost for projects that have been marked as winners at hackathons and Devpost challenges. We then gave 1 point for every award won. This was made by a team of fellow hackathoners: Elijah Elrod, Jim Mirzakhalov, and Willie McClinton. Feel free to contact us with any recommendations for future updates. Let the hacking continue.
